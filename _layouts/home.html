How to get to Vinci?

Whatever transportation you plan to take, don't hesitate to contact us if you have any doubts.
Even better if, in the form below, you let us know if you need a ride and indicate which city you're coming from, or if you have available seats in your car.


WE'RE WAITING FOR YOU IN VINCI !!!


  • If you're driving, exit the FI-PI-LI highway at EMPOLI CENTRO. Follow the signs to Vinci. The Church is located in the center of the town.
  • If you're traveling by train, the nearest train station is in EMPOLI.
    From there, you can take an intercity bus (line 49 EMPOLI-VINCI), but they don't travel very frequently.
    Anyway, let us know your arrival time, and we'll arrange to pick you up at the station!
  • If you're flying, the nearest airports are in Pisa ("Galileo Galilei") or Florence ("Amerigo Vespucci").
    From Florence airport, you can take the T2 tram to Santa Maria Novella station in Florence, and then take a train.
    From Pisa airport, you can take the Pisamover to the central station in Pisa, or enjoy a 15-minute walk to the station.
  • If you're arriving by Flixbus, EMPOLI is a stop. If it's not available, try Florence or Pisa.
